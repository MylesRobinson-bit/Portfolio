Twin Pops — Fall 2025

01 — Brand Advertising

Twin Pops
Campaign

Type Group Project
Course Advanced Principles — UNT
Year Fall 2025

Twin Pops had a real problem: affordable, nostalgic, and widely distributed — but invisible in the marketplace. Our team diagnosed the brand's identity gap and built a full advertising campaign to close it. Anchored in the concept of reclaiming youth and the memories attached to it, we developed the strategic message, defined the visual and photographic direction, and built out executions across multiple touchpoints. The campaign was presented as a complete strategy including SMIT, moodboard, and final creative.

Brand Strategy Campaign Development Creative Direction Audience Research Visual Identity

INFINITI
Music Brand

Type Solo Project
Course Brand Development Lab — UNT
Year Spring 2026

A full brand identity built from scratch for an independent music production label targeting the DFW independent music scene. The project encompassed naming, logo concept, brand story, visual identity, target market definition, brand positioning, and competitive differentiation. Included a SWOT analysis, an integrated marketing campaign ("Feel Something") across social, direct, and experiential channels, and a monetization model built around BeatStars and SoundCloud distribution.
